About Euro Currency

The euro is the second largest reserve currency as well as the second most traded currency in the world after the United States dollar. As of January 2017, with more than €1.1 trillion in circulation, the euro has one of the highest combined values of banknotes and coins in circulation in the world, having surpassed the U.S. dollar

About Renminbi Currency

Renminbi and Yuan are used interchangeably, but "Renminbi" is the "official" term used to refer to China's currency and it literally means "the people's currency". The Chinese Renminbi was the 5th most traded currency in the world in 2015 and 2016

About Peso Currency

The Mexican peso is the 8th most traded currency in the world, the third most traded currency originating from the Americas (after the United States dollar and Canadian dollar), and the most traded currency originating from Latin America.

About Swiss franc Currency

The Swiss Franc has a reputation for being a safe haven or hard currency. It is often purchased during financial uncertainty due to its reliability and stability. Regularly used as a global reserve currency, the Swiss Franc is the sixth most traded currency in the world.
